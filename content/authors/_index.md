Dr. Jian Zhang is an **Associate Chief Physician** (Associate Professor) at the Department of Gastrointestinal Surgery, The Seventh Affiliated Hospital, Sun Yat-sen University.

As a **Surgeon-Scientist**, Dr. Zhang integrates clinical excellence with translational research:

* **Clinical Expertise:** He specializes in minimally invasive radical surgeries (Robotic & Laparoscopic) for gastric and colorectal cancer, having participated in over **700 cases**.
* **Research Focus:** His laboratory investigates the molecular mechanisms of GI tumors, specifically **lncRNAs** and **Nanomedicine**.

Dr. Zhang completed his Ph.D. at **The Chinese University of Hong Kong** and postdoctoral training in the United States.
