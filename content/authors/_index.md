Dr. Jian Zhang is an **Associate Chief Physician** (Associate Professor) at the Department of Gastrointestinal Surgery, The Seventh Affiliated Hospital, Sun Yat-sen University.

As a **Surgeon-Scientist**, Dr. Zhang integrates clinical excellence with translational research:

* **Clinical Expertise:** He specializes in minimally invasive radical surgeries (Robotic & Laparoscopic) for gastric and colorectal cancer, having participated in over **700 cases**. He also manages complex conditions including gastrointestinal perforations and hernias.
* **Research Focus:** His laboratory investigates the molecular mechanisms of GI tumors, specifically:
    1.  **Non-coding RNAs:** The role of lncRNAs (e.g., *GHRLOS*, *FAL1*) in tumor microenvironment and chemoresistance.
    2.  **Nanomedicine:** Development of biomimetic nanoparticles for targeted drug delivery.
    3.  **Cardiotoxicity:** Building the *KICDB* database to study kinase inhibitor-induced cardiotoxicity.

Dr. Zhang completed his Ph.D. at **The Chinese University of Hong Kong** and postdoctoral training in the United States. He has published multiple high-impact papers in journals such as *ACS Nano*, *Oncogene*, and *Frontiers in Oncology*.
